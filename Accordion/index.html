In what type of matter are atoms most tightly packed?

Solid atoms are very tightly packed because atoms are very close to each other. ... Liquids have the arrangement of the atoms looser than that of in solid. ... In gases, atoms are very loosely packed because of the high kinetic energy of atoms in the gas phase. So, gas molecules experience very less force of attraction.

What is the hottest planet in the solar system?

Even though Mercury is closer to the Sun, Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system. Its thick atmosphere is full of the greenhouse gas carbon dioxide, and it has clouds of sulfuric acid.

What color is your blood when it’s inside your body?

It's red because of the red blood cells (hemoglobin). Blood does change color somewhat as oxygen is absorbed and replenished. But it doesn't change from red to blue. It changes from red to dark red.

How many teeth does an adult human have?

The short answer is that humans have 20 primary teeth and 32 secondary (permanent) teeth during their lifetime.

What is the largest planet in the solar system?

Jupiter is the fifth planet from our Sun and is, by far, the largest planet in the solar system – more than twice as massive as all the other planets combined.
